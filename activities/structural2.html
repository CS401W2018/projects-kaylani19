What goes in the header?

Things that are usually found within the header are links or navigation tags that make using the web page easier.

What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?

Links to other webpages or information is what goes in the Nav. Yes the Nav can go in the header. No it doesn't HAVE to go in the header. Yes the Nav contains links to another website.

What goes in Main?

The main content or information that is important for a specific topic/subject.

What goes in the Footer?

The information about the author of the webpage, legal information and other related links.
