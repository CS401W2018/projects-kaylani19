1. What are the positives and negatives of hosting your videos on YouTube?

= Positives of hosting your videos on Youtube would be that Youtube allows you to host videos without paying for storage. Another positive is that you can easily embed Youtube videos on your website by copying the embed code provided and that videos are easily accessible to a wide audience since Youtube is a very popular platform.

= Negatives of hosting videos on Youtube would be that there is a high probability that there will be ads and that some video owners disable embedding, limiting your ability to include specific videos on your website.
