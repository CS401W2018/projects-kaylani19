1. What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tags take up the full width available and start on a new line. Inline tags, only take up as much space as needed and stay within the flow of the text.

2. What is the margin, border, and padding?

Margin is the area outside the border and a border is the area around the padding and content. Padding is the area inside the border, between the content and the border.
