1. The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

= The "action" attribute specifies where to send the form data when the form is submitted.

The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

= The "method" attribute determines how the data is sent to the server, either as part of the URL(GET) or indise the request(POST).

What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?

= "GET" will allow the data be displayed on the URL; whereas "POST" will not be visible in the URL and more secure or sensitive data.
