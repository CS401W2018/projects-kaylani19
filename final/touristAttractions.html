Samoa Tourist Compass

Here's are a few tips and tricks as well as recommendations to improve your experience in Samoa. From hotels to visit, to places to have fun and establishments for you to explore and have a taste of what the Samoan life is like. Explore the iconic landmarks and experiences Samoa has to offer. Immerse yourself in the beauty and culture of this tropical paradise.

Popular Attractions

Commonly Visited Tourist Attractions
Name Location Description Entry Fee
Piula Cave Pools Piula, Upolu A freshwater pool located inside a lava tube, perfect for swimming in clear waters. $5 per person and $10-20 per car (depends on size)
Eveni Carruthers Apia, Upolu Eveni Carruthers is a prominent Samoan apparel brand blending traditional elei designs with modern fashion, known for producing uniforms, casual wear, and globally recognized pieces like the All Blacks' elei shirts. N/A
To-Sua Ocean Trench Lotofaga, Upolu A 30-meter deep volcanic trench filled with seawater, connected by ladders and gardens. $20 per adult
Alogaaga Blowholes Taga, Savai'i Powerful blowholes created by waves forcing water through lava tubes. N/A
Saleaula Lava Fields Saleula, Savai'i Lava remnants from the 1905 Mt. Matavanu eruption, including preserved church ruins. $5 per person
Mount Vaea Scenic Trail Vailima, Upolu A hike leading to the tomb of Robert Louis Stevenson with panoramic views. N/A
