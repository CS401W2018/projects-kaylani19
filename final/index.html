Discover the Beauty of Samoa

Samoa is a group of islands, both big and small, filled with warm hospitality, and deep-rooted culture. Whether you're here to learn about its fascinating history, explore its traditions, or discover hidden treasures, there's something for everyone. Explore and learn about Samoa's history, people, attractions and more.

History

Discover Samoa's rich history, from its colonial struggles and division into Western and American Samoa to its inspiring journey to independence. Learn about pivotal movements like the Mau Movement, key figures such as Tupua Tamasese Lealofi III, and the legacy of leaders who shaped the nation's identity.

Church & Religion

Explore the profound influence of Christianity on Samoan culture and daily life. Did You Know?

  • Samoa was given the name "happy" people by Robert Louis Stevenson, famous writer of Treasure Island, who later settled and died in Samoa
  • Traditional Samoan tattoos known as tatau are a symbol of pride and honor and each pattern has a distinct meaning behind it
  • Samoa lost a day in 2011 to align with trade partners (Australia, New Zealand etc.)
