The Russo Family

Link Link Link Link Link
  • The Russo Family is a family of wizards (not including the parents - Jerry & Theresa Russo)
  • Jerry Russo use to be a wizard but gave up his powers to marry Theresa
  • They live in Manhattan's Greenwich Village area
  • They own and run a subway-themed sandwich shop and have a lair in their freezer
  • Jerry, the father, teaches his kids to prepare for the wizard competition which will determine who will become the next family wizard

Meet the Family

The middle child and only daughter of Theresa and Jerry Russo. She is rebellious, sarcastic, and outgoing. Although she makes daring choices at times, she values her family and comes to understand the importance of her decisions and actions over time.

Justin is the eldest sibling, and is known for his good academics and nerdy behavior. He takes his learning very seriously and tries his best to excel in everything he does. He often butts with Alex but is always willing to help when needed.

Max is the youngest of the Russo children, he has a very playful nature and carefree. Max's unpredictability constanty keeps the family on their toes. Despite his relaxed approach to life, he has a heart of gold and brings a balance to the Russo family.
